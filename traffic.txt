How the flow of traffic goes through each port on the


1. Command runs to access VM on
2. Traffic goes from my machine to port 8080 on the VM (the top layer)
3. Traffic then goes from the VM to the Kind cluster which listens on port 30950
4. It then goes from the Kind cluster to the kubernetes service port on port 80
5. Then from the service to the pod ie the apps container on port 5000
6. Then to the flask app itself on port 5000, the flask app is listening on all ports but receives into port 5000 default
